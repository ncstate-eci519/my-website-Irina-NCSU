Gamification Tools:

Below is the list of my favorite gamification platforms:

  • Gimkit. An innovative quiz learning platform made as an engaging gameplay.
  • Kahoot! One of the most popular game-based learning platforms. It has easy-to-use features and a free version.
  • Go Noodle. This platform features hundreds of videos designed to get kids moving.
  • Book Creator. This is a great tool for students to explore their creative side. The app alows making ebooks on an iPad.
