Learning Management Systems

According to Wikipedia, Learning Management System (LMS) is a "software application or web-based technology that ranges from managing trining and training records to distributing courses to employees/students over the Internet."

On my Home Page I have provided the list of my favorite LMS. To see the full list of the best e-learning systems, please visit ELearning Industry.

Below is the list of my most favorite LMS platforms:

  • Teachable. This online platform is easy to learn and personalize
  • Storyline 360. The classics of e-learning systems.
  • Canvas. Another "classic" LMS with a great number of features.
  • Wakelet. This e-learning platform allows to save and organize all kinds of online content.
