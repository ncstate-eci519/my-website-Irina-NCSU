Whiteboard Applications

Below is the list of my favofite whiteboard apps:

  • GoBoard. This is a free online tool that combines video chatting and an interactive canvas.
  • ShowMe. A free, easy-to-use whiteboard app for an iPad.
  • Explain Everything. An interactive app that allows you to animate, record, annotate, and collaborate in real time.
