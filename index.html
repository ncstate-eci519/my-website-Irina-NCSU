I am not an educator per se, but sometimes I prepare tutorials and mini-courses for our clients. Below is the list of my favorite edtech tools.

My Favorite EdTech Tools

I have devided my favorite edtech tools into 4 broad categories: LMS, Gamification Platforms, Communication Tools, and Whiteboard Apps.

Learning Management Systems (LMS)

  • Teachable. This online platform is easy to learn and personalize
  • Storyline 360. The classics of e-learning systems.
  • Canvas. Another "classic" LMS with a great number of features.
  • Wakelet. This e-learning platform allows to save and organize all kinds of online content.
To read more about Learning Management Systems, go to Page 2

Gamification Platforms

  • Gimkit. An innovative quiz learning platform made as an engaging gameplay.
  • Kahoot! One of the most popular game-based learning platforms. It has easy-to-use features and a free version.
  • Go Noodle. This platform features hundreds of videos designed to get kids moving.
  • Book Creator. This is a great tool for students to explore their creative side. The app alows making ebooks on an iPad.

Communication Tools

  • Remind. Classroom messaging app for quick and efficient comunication between teachers, students, and parents.
  • Loop. This app is for teachers to collect structured feedback from students. It can be integrated with Google Clasroom./li>
  • Living Tree. This is a great social networking platform for a school community.

Whiteboard Apps

  • GoBoard. This is a free online tool that combines video chatting and an interactive canvas.
  • ShowMe. A free, easy-to-use whiteboard app for an iPad.
  • Explain Everything. An interactive app that allows you to animate, record, annotate, and collaborate in real time.
